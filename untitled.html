doc-pagenum

A visible indicator of the page number.

The doc-pagenum role MUST be used on visible page numbers present in a doc-pageheader or doc-pagefooter. See those two roles for examples of doc-pagenum usage.

An alternate way of indicating the page number, when it is not visible in the header or footer of the page, is to use the title attribute on a doc-pagebreak.
